Rules

Rolling the dice

  • Rolls reduce fuel consumption by roll. Distance traveled is roll x3.
  • Rolling a two 1s reduces players fuel to zero and penalizes player for 3 turns
  • Rolling one 1 reduces players fuel by 25%
  • Rolling two 6s adds 50 laps and no fuel penalty

Refueling

  • Lossing all fuel penalizes players for 3 turns.
  • If fuel is less than 50% then players get 50 fuel.
  • If fuel is greater than 50% then players refuel to 100%.
  • Penalty turns add no fuel.
